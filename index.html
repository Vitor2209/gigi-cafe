About Us

At Gigi Café, we serve freshly brewed coffee, handcrafted drinks, and delicious food in a warm and welcoming environment in Norwood. A perfect spot to relax, work, or enjoy time with friends.

Our passion for quality coffee drives everything we do. From carefully sourced beans to expertly crafted drinks, every cup tells a story of dedication and love for the craft.
